Shaping engaging experiences

You learn. We create. They use.

We are a User Experience (UX) design consultancy that specializes in creating and improving digital products for all types of companies and organizations.

What we do

Experience Design

Everything from improving your existing website to designing a new app from scratch. We design the flow, and every screen of your product or service, using our agile and lean UX process. Your users will immediately be able to do what they need to do.

Usability Testing

We test how satisfied your customers are with your product or service. We believe in testing at every stage in the design process and we use a wide variety of methods. From learning by observing to analyzing the data of 100’s of customers as they use your product.

Training

Our trainings and workshops are specifically tailored to each client. From helping with a specific project or subject (User Testing, Design Collaboration Workshops) to streamlining the UX process within your company (Interim UX Lead, Agile UX, Lean UX).

Our process

  • Learn

  • Create

  • Use

Together we define your goals, Learn about your business, your customers and the competitive landscape of your industry. We then create a UX strategy for your product, service or software.

Our methods include:

Customer research, clients workshops, competitive analysis, heuristic and expert evaluations.

We Create products your customers love. Applying the science of UX design to create an experience which meets their needs. Programming is expensive and we ensure you code just what is valuable to your customers and your business.

Our methods include:

Sketchwork, personas and user stories, experience maps, style guides, rapid prototyping.

We design the ease-of-use in close collaboration with the development team, and we continue testing and learning more about your customers as they Use your product or service. This ensures your product continues to improve once shipped and your customer retention is maximised.

Our methods include:

Learning labs, usability testing, A/B testing, user surveys and web analytics.
