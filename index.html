What we do

We are a User Experience (UX) agency that specializes in baking emotionally engaging experiences. Our skilled multi-cultural team loves getting to know your users to understand their unique motivations, expectations and behaviors. This knowledge is then shaped into intuitive and engaging experience design.

We enjoy working with all type of companies and organisations: start-ups, global corporations, advertising agencies, non-profits, and everything in between.

We work with clients worldwide. In English, French, Italian and Spanish. 

Our Services Include:

  • Customer or end-user research

  • Design collaboration workshops

  • Interaction design and wireframe development

  • Lean UX for start-ups

  • Personas and user scenarios

  • Proof of concept prototyping

  • Usability evaluation of your products or services

  • User testing and analysis
